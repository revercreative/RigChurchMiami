Active Initiatives

What We're Building

Every initiative is an extension of our call to be Kings and Priests - people who don't just occupy space, but transform it.

● Active Initiative

Back to School Backpack Project

We are collecting and distributing fully stocked backpacks for children in our community ahead of the new school year. Every child who walks into a classroom prepared is a child set up to succeed. Donations of backpacks, school supplies, and funds are welcomed - and volunteers are needed to help pack and distribute.

Children's Learning Center Expansion

Rig Church Miami is expanding its Children's Learning Center to serve more families with quality early education, tutoring, and development programs. This is one of our most significant investments in the next generation - and we are looking for partners who share that vision.

Meet the Team

Our Leadership

Rooted in the Word and led by the Spirit. Meet the pastors and leaders who serve Rig Church Miami.

Senior Pastors

Pastors Elijah

& Manille Colas

Rooted in the Word and led by the Spirit, Pastors Elijah and Manille Colas founded Rig Church Miami with a vision to cultivate disciples who encounter God daily - to Believe in Christ, Belong in Community, and Build up the Church and City.

Care Pastors

Pastors Ryan & Camille Aldophe

The Care Department shepherds our church family, providing support, guidance, and encouragement in every season of life - through Celebrations & Crisis Care, Freedom Groups, and Counseling.

Spiritual Formation Pastors

Pastors Laurence & Kesley Smith

The Spiritual Formation team helps individuals grow deeper in their relationship with God - guiding our church family in hearing God's voice, walking in spiritual clarity, and maturing in faith through Prophetic One on One, Dream & Vision Submission, and Discipleship.

Family & Life Pastors

Pastors Wilson & Shantel Merantus

The Family & Life Department equips and supports every stage of life - investing in relationships, personal growth, and family discipleship through Youth, Men & Women, and Marriage ministry.
